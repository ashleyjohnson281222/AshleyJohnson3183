Fernando's Authentic Cuisine is proud to welcome our award-winning Chef Claw, straight from world famous Michelin Star Academy in London England! Finishing at the top 5 of his class, Chef Claw was offered a job in Hell's kitchen by Gordan Ramsey but turned him down to travel and gain knowledge of food from all different cultures. Chef Lob Ster Claw won several awards, The Michelin Star Award, James Beard Award and Best Chef voted by Chefs. Chef Claw has over 20 years of experience in fine dining and has traveled around the world learning all different cuisines.
