Welcome! Thank you for visiting Fernando's Authentic Cuisine, located at the heart of Red Bank New Jersey. In business since 1997, we are known for our variety of cuisines and world-famous dishes. Come enjoy delicious high-end cuisine from multiple countries across the world.

Choose from our indoor dining area to our beautiful outdoor views of the Navesink River. Fernando's has been renowned for our world class five-star rating. Many our chefs have been recruited straight from world famous Michelin Star in London, England. Our chefs are trained in world class cuisines from all around the world. They are trained from European to South American and Asian cuisines.

We do indoor and outdoor catering, host all kinds of parties in our ballroom area as well as wedding reception services. For our customers desiring our high-end delicacies but cannot make it due to our high volume of customers or reservation availability or you just want to order in, we also deliver right to your door step with a smile and restaurant quality food!
