When we opened Fernando's Authentic Cuisine we were inspired by my husband and my own heritage. In 1992, we decided we would take a year long trip visiting all the places of our heritage. On our yearlong adventure, we enjoyed food from many different cultures. When we came back home to New Jersey we decided to take all the different types of food that we enjoyed from other countries. We opened Fernando's in hopes that we could give every client a taste of our adventure.
